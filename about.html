About Saudi Events

Our Story

Saudi Events was founded in 2025 with a vision to showcase the growing cultural and entertainment scene in the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia. What started as a small initiative to list local events in Riyadh has grown into the nation's leading platform for discovering, booking, and experiencing the best events across the Kingdom.

As Saudi Arabia undergoes its ambitious transformation under Vision 2030, we're proud to play a part in highlighting the rich tapestry of entertainment options now available to residents and visitors alike. From traditional cultural festivals to international concerts, sporting events to theatrical performances, Saudi Events is your gateway to experiencing the new Saudi Arabia.

Our Mission

To connect people with memorable experiences by providing the most comprehensive, user-friendly platform for discovering and attending events throughout Saudi Arabia.

Our Vision

To become the premier events platform in the Middle East, known for innovation, reliability, and our contribution to Saudi Arabia's cultural and entertainment sectors.

Our Values

Integrity

We operate with transparency and honesty in all our dealings with users, partners, and event organizers.

Community

We foster a sense of belonging and connection through shared experiences and cultural appreciation.

Innovation

We continuously seek new ways to improve our platform and enhance the user experience.

Quality

We are committed to excellence in every aspect of our service, from event curation to customer support.

Our Journey

2025/3/5

The Beginning

Saudi Events was founded by a small team of tech enthusiasts and event lovers in Riyadh, initially covering only local events in the capital city.

2025/3/20

Expanding Horizons

Expanded operations to include Jeddah, Dammam, and AlUla, partnering with event organizers across the Kingdom to provide comprehensive event listings.

2025/3/25

Digital Transformation

Launched our mobile app and implemented an integrated ticketing system, making it easier for users to discover and attend events.

2025/4/10

Strategic Partnerships

Formed key partnerships with the General Entertainment Authority, Ministry of Culture, and major event producers in the region.

2025/4/15

Innovation & Growth

Introduced AI-powered recommendations and virtual event options, reaching over 1 million active users.

2025/5/10

Going Regional

Expanded services to neighboring GCC countries while maintaining our focus on showcasing the best of Saudi cultural and entertainment events.

2025/5/15

Recognition & Excellence

Recognized as the leading events platform in Saudi Arabia, with over 5,000 events listed annually and 3 million active users.

2025/5/25

The Future Beckons

Continuing to innovate and grow, with new features and services being developed to better serve our community of event-goers and organizers.
